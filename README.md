Serpulo+ is a personalized content expansion mod for Mindustry, focused on adding Serpulo content. 
Features:
- Tier 2 and Tier 3 upgrades for vanilla crafters (examples: Silicon Refinery as the Tier 3 of the Silicon Smelter, and Plastanium Infuser as the Tier 2 of the Plastanium Compressor)
- The Alloy Trio: Neoplastic, Cryogenic & Ultimatter Alloys. Neoplastic Alloy created in the Neoplastic Smelter, Cryogenic & Ultimatter Alloys created in the Cryogenic Freezer, both blocks having their own Tier 2 and Tier 3 variants.
- Neoplasm is brought to Serpulo through the Cell Incubator.
- Expanded Scrap processing blocks, featuring the Scrap Melder, Specific Separators, and the Heavy Incinerator.
- Liquid Capsules, a new way of liquid transportation created within Capsule Assemblers and filled/emptied with Liquid Encapsulators and Decapsulators, respectively.
- 2 new cores: Core: Scout and Core: Apex.
- 5 new turrets: Tesla, Vitreous, Randren, Rowhammer, and Stuxnet.
---
